Welcome to the JamRock Soccer Cup Page

This Soccer League will run for 2 months and compromises of 8 teams from the surrounding neighborhoods.

Matches are scheduled for Saturdays and Sundays only (2 matches per day), 9 a.m and 12 p.m .

Each team will play a total of 7 matches (each team will play against all the other teams in the league once).

At the end of the League the team with the most points wil be awarded a Trophy along with other prizes.
