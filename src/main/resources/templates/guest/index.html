Welcome to the RUBIS Soccer Cup Fan Page


This Soccer League is played throughout the Fall and compromises of 8 teams from the surrounding neighborhoods.

Matches are scheduled for Saturdays and Sundays only (2 matches per day), 1 p.m and 4 p.m .

Each team will play a total of 7 matches (each team will play against all the other teams in the league once).

At the end of the League the team with the most points wil be awarded a Trophy along with other prizes.
